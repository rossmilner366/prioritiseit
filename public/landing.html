Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Purpose-built for prioritisation. Not a project management suite, not a roadmap tool — just the scoring and sharing part, done properly.

RICE, ICE & WSJF scoring

Choose your framework per board. Auto-calculated scores with live re-ranking. Built-in scoring guide so your team scores consistently.

Stakeholder sharing

One click generates a read-only link. Stakeholders see the ranked list and matrix — no login required. Revoke access any time.

Impact/effort matrix

Visual 2×2 scatter plot with Quick Wins, Big Bets, Fill-ins, and Avoid quadrants. Export as PNG for your slide deck.

Team collaboration

Invite colleagues as editors or viewers. Score features together, drag to reorder, and see changes in real time.

Drag-and-drop override

Override the calculated ranking when you know something the formula doesn't. Pinned items are visually flagged so stakeholders can see the judgement call.

Link to specs & designs

Attach a URL to each feature — Figma files, Google Docs, Confluence pages, PowerPoint decks. Everything in context.

How it works

Three steps to clarity

1

Add your features

List the features, ideas, or initiatives you're considering. Add as many as you need.

2

Score them

Rate each feature on Reach, Impact, Confidence, and Effort. The app calculates and ranks automatically.

3

Share the link

One click generates a stakeholder-ready view. No login needed. Never argue about "why X before Y" again.
